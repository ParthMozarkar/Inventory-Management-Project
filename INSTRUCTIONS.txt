--- [ INSTALLATION INSTRUCTIONS ] ---

1.  This is a Portable Application. NO INSTALLATION REQUIRED.
2.  Simply copy the "VJ_Beer_Inventory.exe" file to any folder on your computer (e.g., Desktop or Documents).
3.  Double-click "VJ_Beer_Inventory.exe" to launch.

--- [ FIRST RUN ] ---

-   The application will automatically create a "db" folder next to the .exe file. 
    This is where your database (IEEE_Shop.db) is stored. 
-   DO NOT DELETE the "db" folder, or you will lose your data!
